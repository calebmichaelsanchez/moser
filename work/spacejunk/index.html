Project: Spacejunk
Service: Website Redesign
Role: Lead Designer / Art Direction

Spacejunk is a live action, motion graphics, and animation studio based in Columbus, Ohio. They wanted to move beyond their previous 'space' themed website, and were in search of a more sophisticated way to present their vast range of work. More importantly, they wanted to provide users with a closer look into what sets their work apart from their competitors: the people, their processes, and the special culture they've created.
