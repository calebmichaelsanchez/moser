Project: Travels - India
Role: Teacher / Caretaker / Adventurer

Between 2009-2010 I had the privilege to visit a great friend, Rocky Braat, in a small village 2 hours south of Chennai, India. Rocky works there full time as a caretaker for HIV+ women and children. I was fortunate enough to join him in his work for a short time. What I experienced in that time has forever shaped my perspectives on living a meaningful life while setting and impactful example through love and positivity.
