Project: Travels - India
Role: Teacher / Caretaker / Adventurer

Between 2009-2010 I had the priviledge to visit a great friend, Rocky Braat, in a small village 2 hours South of Chennai, India. Rocky works there full time as a caretaker for HIV positive women and children. I was fortunate enough to join him in his work for a short time. What I experienced in that has forever shaped my perspectives on living a meaningful life and setting an impactful example through love and positivity.
