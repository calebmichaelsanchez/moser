Project: Salon Lofts
Service: Website Redesign
Role: Lead Designer

Salon lofts is a connector brand. They supply beauty specialists with the space and tools they need to run their own businesses. They also provide the pathway for consumers to book those specialists. The main goal of this redesign was to enhance the user's experience by ensuring the quickest route to making those connections.
